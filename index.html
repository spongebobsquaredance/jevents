About

JaDa Events is an event management company that is a partnership between Jan Haughey, CMP and David Scher.

JaDa Events recognizes the need to bring together professional organizations with the same needs and concerns into a single event.

JaDa Events has the experience and ability to organize and execute successful conferences and trade shows to any industry segment in need of professional assistance looking to obtain optimum total event results.

Who We Are

Jan and Dave have worked together since 2001 for a Massachusetts based audio-visual integrator, where they developed educational programs for their clients, and events to facilitate the need for their suppliers to get face time with the end-users.

JAN HAUGHEY, CMP
FOUNDER | Video Educators of New England (VENE)
CO-FOUNDER | The New England Broadcast & Cable Expo

Jan brings more than 25 years of experience working with clients in the broadcast and audio-visual presentation markets.

Jan founded VENE to help her clients (primarily video educators at regional schools/colleges and studio management for cable access stations) have a place to meet, learn about new technology and share new ideas.

In 2011 VENE was renamed Media Educators of America (MEOA) in recognition of the change in technology and integration of many forms of media in the education of visual communication.

DAVE SHER
CO-FOUNDER | The New England Broadcast & Cable Expo
PRESIDENT/OWNER | Basics Marketing

Dave brings over 20 years of experience in promotion and marketing of businesses and their products.
